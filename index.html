Step 1. Understanding the Problem Statement

Sesame is a patient-centered healthcare startup focused on connecting patients to affordable care. A key part of its digital platform is a web-based medical content (blogs and videos) hub where patients can access high-quality health information. The target users are cost-conscious patients seeking credible provider options and providers looking to attract visits through the platform. Currently, Sesame aims to address three main design issues:

  • For patients: increase the readability and accessibility of medical content
  • For providers: improve the content creation experience through AI assistance and account growth tracking
  • For both: create effective channels that convert patients into provider visits

The ultimate goal is to deliver clear and trustworthy health information that guides patients to care options, while boosting provider engagement and visibility. It is hence important that our design satisfies the needs of both groups.

Step 2. Sketching and Wireframing

Now that we understand the problems, we can brainstorm ideas and create sketches. Below are four sets of sketches covering five key pages we believe are essential to solving these issues in the content hub: a blog homepage, an article page, a provider dashboard, and AI-assisted blog and video creation pages. The sketches include a range of designs, such as different AI interaction modes and CTAs (call-to-action elements) on the blog and dashboard pages.

ADELA'S PART - WHAT IS KEPT FROM SKETCHES

Page 1: Blog Homepage

  • Diversifies the content exploration experience through interactive elements like a search bar, featured article cards, and topic-based browsing
  • Improves content readability by adopting a modular, visually scannable design with clear hiearchies instead of a text-heavy layout

Page 2: Individual Article Page

  • Diversifies the content consumption experience by offering an additional video format
  • Creates effective conversion channels by featuring a floating provider section with direct links to profile, appointment booking, and messaging
  • Enhances content readability through a clean and article-centered layout

Page 3: Provider Dashboard

  • Improves the content contribution experience by visualizing metrics for content visibility and account growth tracking
  • Increases content creation efficiency through quick-access actions and a structured draft system that streamlines providers' workflow

Page 4: AI Assistant Page

  • Enhances the content creation experience through a creative human-AI co-creation format and interactive canvases for content editing
  • Increases creation efficiency by guided AI assistance throughout the process, from idea brainstorming to draft refinement and fact-checking
